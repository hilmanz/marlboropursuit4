HOW TO PLAY Doubt Crasher

Arrow key designations: LEFT JUMP RIGHT

You're playing as "Doubt Crasher". You will run forward no matter what. Make him change lanes & jump using the arrow buttons.

Break through the "MAYBE's" while running past motivational words and phrases.

Smashing through the "MAYBE" word or phrase earns you 10 points while crashing into motivational words and phrases gets you a penalty of -5 points.

Reach the minimum number of points to play the next level.
