MARLBORO.PH : THE PURSUIT 2013
OFFICIAL PROMOTION MECHANICS

  1. GENERAL
    1. The Pursuit promotion ("Promotion") period is from July 1 to September 9, 2013.
    2. No purchase necessary to register but some tasks may require cigarette pack purchase. To be part of the promotion, a participant must:
      1. be a Filipino citizen
      2. be an adult smoker, 18 years or older as of July 1, 2013
      3. be part of the MARLBORO M List
      4. be of sound mind and body

    Adult (min. 18 years old) smokers who wish to be part of the MARLBORO M List may register online through www.marlboro.ph ("Website") by completing the online registration form and submitting a copy of his/her valid government-issued ID (GIID) that shows his/her date of birth (i.e. Passport, Driver's License, Tax Identification Card with Picture, LTO Student's Permit, SSS ID with Birthdate, Unified Multi-Purpose ID, Postal ID, Voter's ID, NBI Clearance, Police Clearance, Alien Certificate of Registration). An age verification process shall be conducted by representatives of PMFTC Inc. ("PMFTC") within seven (7) days of registration.

    1. Adult (min. 18 years old) smokers who wish to be part of the MARLBORO M List may also register offline through MARLBORO Brand Hostesses that visit selected partner bars, clubs, and restaurants. The adult (min. 18 years old) smoker must be ready to show sufficient evidence of age such as a government-issued ID with his/her date of birth as proof of age to complete the registration.
    2. An email notification containing his/her login credentials to the Website shall be sent to the adult (min. 18 years old) smoker to signify successful registration to the MARLBORO M List. The age-verified adult (min. 18 years old) smoker must log on to the Website using said login credentials to join the Promotion.
    3. Once submitted, all registration forms (online and offline) become the property of PMFTC, including the right to edit, publish, and use the information therein without any consideration or payment to the participant. Registration forms will not be acknowledged or returned.
    4. Grand prize winners of the Promotion will be announced during The Pursuit Send-Off Event ("Event"). The final date and venue of the Event will be announced on a later date.
  2. JOINING "THE PURSUIT" PROMOTION
    1. A participant may join the Promotion by going to The Pursuit section of the Website.
    2. Each participant must collect a set of letters to form the phrase "DON'T BE A MAYBE".
    3. To collect letters, the participant must complete a series of tasks that may be done on the Website or on-ground at various identified locations. The task list will be available online at The Pursuit section of the Website.
    4. Each task completed will entitle the participant to one (1) letter.
    5. PMFTC reserves the right to award bonus/additional letters as deemed fit.
  3. COLLECTION OF LETTERS
    1. For specific offline tasks, a participant must show proof of accomplishment through any of the following:
      1. Uploading a photo on "The Pursuit" section under the specific task they accomplished
      2. Having their photo taken by authorized PMFTC representatives
      3. Inputting a code provided by PMFTC or its authorized representatives on "The Pursuit" section under the specific task they accomplished
    2. Photos are subject to the approval of PMFTC or its authorized representatives prior to being published on the Website.
    3. Codes are subject to the approval of PMFTC or its authorized representatives.
    4. Once proof of a task has been verified and approved, a random letter from "DON'T BE A MAYBE" will be released to the participant through the Website.
  4. LETTER TRADING
    1. A participant can trade letters with other participants via The Pursuit's online trading section.
    2. Only letters that have been tagged for trading may be viewed by other participants looking to trade letters.
    3. The participant must choose from his/her roster of letters a letter to trade out and indicate what letter he/she is looking for. The system shall show a list of participants who match his/her trade request.
    4. Both participants must drag the letters they want to trade into the trading section and click confirm to finalize the trade.
    5. Traded letters are considered final and returning of letters is not allowed.
    6. A participant may trade letters with other participants as many times as he/she wants.

  6. SELECTION OF GRAND PRIZE WINNERS
    1. Participants who are able to complete the phrase "DON'T BE A MAYBE" are qualified for a chance to win one (1) of fifteen (15) seats available to participate in MSCAPE, which will be held in Abu Dhabi on November 1 to 3, 2013
    2. Qualified participants will be contacted by authorized representatives of PMFTC via phone call and advised to send the following documents:
      1. Copy of a valid Philippine passport
      2. Three (3) passport-sized photographs
      3. Medical clearance issued by a duly licensed physician certifying that he/she is fit to travel
    3. The first one hundred (100) participants to submit complete documents will undergo an interview and/or tests via phone call or face-to-face and thirty (30) participants will be selected to join in The Pursuit's finals round.
    4. These thirty (30) finalists will be contacted by PMFTC through telegram, phone call and/or email to advise them of their selection and their participation in the finals round.
    5. Any qualifier who has not responded to the telegram, phone call and/or email conducted by PMFTC within five (5) days of notification will be disqualified and the next available qualifier will be chosen to take their place. If these other qualifiers cannot be contacted as well, then these vacant slots will be forfeited, and the process will proceed even with an incomplete line up.
    6. The thirty (30) finalists will be brought to Metro Manila to participate in a final challenge that will determine the winners of The Pursuit. A panel of judges, determined by PMFTC, will be present during the final challenge. The selection and/or any decision of the judges will be considered final. In the event that a selected finalist is not available, he/she will be disqualified and the next highest placed finalist will be selected. Non Metro Manila residents will be provided with appropriate transportation and accommodations.
    7. Fifteen (15) winners will be selected and awarded a seat to participate in MSCAPE at Abu Dhabi, Dubai. MSCAPE will consist of 3 days of activities such as attending a Formula1™ race, partying and exploring the culture of the country. The trip will be on October 31 to November 5, 2013.
    8. Each participant must be available on these designated dates. In the event that a selected participant is not available, his/her slot will be forfeited and the next highest placed participant in The Pursuit based on the aforementioned criteria will take his/her place.
    9. PMFTC will provide round trip air transportation to and from the MSCAPE destination, accommodations, regular daily meals, local transportation, and other provisions as determined by PMFTC
    10. Redeemed prizes and corresponding invitation to participate in the MSCAPE in Abu Dhabi are not transferable and not convertible to cash or other goods. No substitutes are permitted. PMFTC will be responsible for any taxes that a participant may incur by reason of his her participation in the MSCAPE. The participants will be fully responsible for the payment of any expenses of participation not specifically covered by the Official Mechanics.
    11. PMFTC reserves the right to change dates, locations, and events comprised in the 2013 MSCAPE in Abu Dhabi or to cancel in whole or in part if prevented or restricted from conducting the program for reasons outside the control of PMFTC, without compensation to the selected participants.
    12. In the event that the MSCAPE in Abu Dhabi is cancelled in its entirety, PMFTC may, without notice, substitute any prize with comparable value.
    13. Prior to participating in the MSCAPE in Abu Dhabi, all participants will be required to execute or furnish such documentation as PMFTC may require including, but not limited to, a good health report, complete liability release, indemnification and waiver, exclusivity contract and complete publicity release and undertaking. They must also undergo and pass a medical examination to be conducted by a PMFTC appointed physician and be inoculated against tetanus. Written proof that a participant meets the required qualifications, is a holder of a valid Philippine passport and has executed the required documentation, must be furnished to PMFTC before departure for Abu Dhabi. Failure to do so will result in the selection of a replacement.
    14. Qualified selected participants to the MSCAPE in Abu Dhabi must also be eligible for and successfully secure a visa for Dubai. PMFTC does not undertake and guarantee the issuance of a visa to Dubai for any of the participants. It will be the sole responsibility of the selected participants to secure their respective visas.
    15. PMFTC, however, will provide the selected participants with a written certification pertinent to their participation to MSCAPE in Abu Dhabi and its corresponding invitation to participate in other activities in Abu Dhabi.
    16. Non-eligibility and failure to secure the above mentioned travel documents in time for departure to Abu Dhabi will result in the selection of a replacement.
    17. All expenditures (i.e. transportation, lodging, fees etc.) that may be incurred in the procurement of their respective visas will be shouldered by the participants.
