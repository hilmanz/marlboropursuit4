MARLBORO DESIGN-YOUR-OWN SHIRT OFFICIAL MECHANICS

2013 MARLBORO.PH PROMOTION

I. General

  1. The MARLBORO Design-Your-Own ("DYO") Shirt promotion ("Promotion") period is from May 20 to June 30, 2013
  2. No purchase necessary. To be part of the promotion, a participant must:
    • be a Filipino citizen
    • be an adult smoker, 18 years or older as of May 20, 2013
    • be part of the MARLBORO M List
  3. Adult (min. 18 years old) smokers who wish to be part of the MARLBORO M List may register online through www.marlboro.ph ("Website") by completing the online registration form and submitting a copy of his/her valid government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, SSS ID, etc.) that shows his/her date of birth. An age verification process shall be conducted by representatives of PMFTC Inc.(PMFTC) within seven (7) days of registration.
  4. Adult (min. 18 years old) smokers who wish to be part of the MARLBORO M List may also register offline through MARLBORO Brand Promoters visiting selected partner Legal Age Meeting Places ("LAMP") such as bars, clubs, and restaurants. The adult (min. 18 years old) smoker must be ready to show sufficient evidence of age such as a government-issued ID with his/her date of birth as proof of age to complete the registration.
  5. An email notification containing his/her login credentials to the Website shall be sent to the adult (min. 18 years old) smoker to signify successful registration to the MARLBORO M List. The age-verified adult (min. 18 years old) smoker must log on to the Website using said login credentials to join the Promotion
  6. Once submitted, all registration forms (online and offline) become the property of PMFTC, including the right to edit, publish, and use the information therein without any consideration or payment to the participant. Registration forms will not be acknowledged or returned

II. JOINING THE "DYO SHIRT" PROMOTION

  1. A participant may join the Promotion by going to the DYO Shirt section of the Website to design his/her own shirt by mixing and matching several pre-approved statements and designs into a shirt of their choice
  2. Each participant may submit a maximum of 3 entries throughout the duration of the promotion period. However, each participant may only win once throughout the duration of the promotion
  3. Entries are subject to the approval of PMFTC or its authorized representatives prior to being published on the Website
  4. Participants may vote online for the best DYO Shirt by "Liking" a shirt design. A participant may not vote on their own entry
  5. One (1) winner will be announced per week. The winner will be selected based on the following criteria:
    • 40% - online votes
    • 60% - offline judging by authorized representatives of PMFTC (only the top 20 entries based on online votes will be judged)
  6. PMFTC reserves the right to add or delete elements from the pre-defined roster as deemed fit
  7. The following schedule will be followed:
Week Duration Deadline of Submission
1 May 20 to 26 May 26, 2013, 11:59 PM
2 May 27 to June 2 June 2, 2013, 11:59 PM
3 June 3 to 9 June 9, 2013, 11:59 PM
4 June 10 to 16 June 16, 2013, 11:59 PM
5 June 17 to 23 June 23, 2013, 11:59 PM
6 June 24 to 30 June 30, 2013, 11:59 PM

III. PRIZE REDEMPTION

  1. The winning design per week shall be produced and awarded to the winning participant who designed that shirt. Only the six (6) winning designs from the DYO Shirt promotion will be produced
  2. A winner's notification email will be sent to the participant's registered email address upon announcement of the weekly winner
  3. Prizes will only be awarded to the participant whose name is registered in the MARLBORO M List and appears in the email notification
  4. To claim the prize, the participant must present the original email notification message (print out) from PMFTC, two (2) original and valid identification documents (i.e. driver's license, passport, and tax identification number (TIN) card). The IDs presented (singly or collectively) should show the following information:
    • Full Name
    • Address
    • Date of birth (confirming that the participant is 18 years or older as of May 20, 2013)
    • Signature

    If the IDs submitted do not contain the four (4) pieces of information, it will not be accepted as valid proof of identification.

  5. In case the winner is unable to present two (2) original and valid IDs acceptable to PMFTC, he/she must submit sworn affidavits, executed by two (2) disinterested persons attesting to his/her identity; and a certification from the responsible municipality/city official certifying that he/she is a resident of the said municipality/city
  6. In cases wherein the winner cannot personally claim his/her prize for any reason, he/she can appoint an adult proxy, 18 years or older, on his/her behalf. The adult proxy should be able to present the following:
    • A letter of authorization duly signed by the winner, nominating the adult proxy as the acknowledged recipient of the prize on the winner's behalf
    • Two (2) original and valid IDs of the winner (singly or collectively showing the required information as mentioned in item III.C.)
    • Two (2) original and valid IDs of the adult proxy
    • Original notification message (e-mail print out) from PMFTC
  7. PMFTC will verify the identity and age of the winning participants before awarding the prizes. Winners who fail the verification procedure (e.g. age non-eligibility) shall be informed of their ineligibility to claim/receive the prize
  8. Prizes are non-transferable and non-convertible to cash.
  9. Prizes will be supplied with standard manufacturer's/dealers' warranties only, where available. PMFTC and its agents are not liable for any warranty whatsoever, over the prize/product, whish is the sole liability of the manufacturer/dealer of the prize/product. PMFTC and its agents will not be liable with respect to: (i) any death or injury or consequential loss or damage arising from the use of any prize; and (ii) any manufacturer's/dealer's refusal to honor any warranty
  10. Prizes are subject to availability and supplier's restrictions. PMFTC may, without notice, withdraw or substitute any prize with comparable value and nature
  11. PMFTC will only shoulder final withholding taxes on prizes amounting to more than PhP10,000. Except as expressly provided herein, PMFTC will not be liable to the winners for any taxes, costs, liabilities or expenses that may arise out of ownership or use of the prizes
